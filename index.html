About Me

Noah Arnold is currently a freshman at Miami University in Oxford Ohio. He is studying Games and Simulation and Computer Science. He graduated from Homestead High School in 2023, graduating with an Honors Diploma. He participated in Boy Scouts throughout middle and high School, eventually earning the rank of Eagle Scout. Currently, Noah competes on the Academic Quiz Team for Miami University. Noah is also an active member of the Anime Club and the Pokemon Club at Miami University. In his free time, Noah enjoys reading fantasy and sci-fi novels, working on new game ideas, and developing new plotlines for his Dungeons and Dragons campaign.
